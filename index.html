FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Acadex?
Simply click "Get Started Free" and create your account. Choose your role (Student, Faculty, or Admin), and you'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard within seconds.
Can I switch between roles?
Each account has one role assigned during registration. If you need a different role, you can create a separate account. Contact support to upgrade or change your role.
Are the certificates globally recognized?
Acadex certificates are issued upon course completion and recognized by our industry partners. Each certificate has a unique verification code. Pro and Enterprise plans include certificates.
Is there a mobile app?
Acadex is fully responsive and works perfectly on all mobile browsers. A dedicated native app is coming soon for iOS and Android.
Can I try the platform before subscribing?
Yes! Our Free plan lets you explore the platform with access to 5 courses. Pro plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.
